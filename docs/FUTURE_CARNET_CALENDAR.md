# Future: 365 calendar in the Carnet

**Status:** Document only. No implementation in arche-paris yet.

## Concept

A 365-day calendar in the Carnet (notebook), starting when the user first enters their code (card activation). The calendar would offer a "different feeling through space and time" — a way to see the year as it unfolds from the moment they join ARCHÉ.

## Design notes

- **Start date:** First card activation (or first journal entry) defines day 1.
- **Content:** Could show walk entries, traces, inscriptions, or simply presence by day (minimal marks, no pressure).
- **Feeling:** "Space and time" suggests the calendar is not just a log but a way to feel the passage of days in relation to Paris and to the user’s own rhythm.
