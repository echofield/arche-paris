# ARCHÉ Archetypal Compass (Non-Negotiable)

## 1) Emotional Operating System

These four emotions are system-level targets. They are not UX decorations.

- `Unveiling`
  - The world was already alive before the walker.
- `Stewardship`
  - Responsibility replaces ownership.
- `Inheritance`
  - The walker enters a continuity larger than self.
- `Refusal`
  - Meaning cannot be forced.

Design consequence:
- We do not optimize engagement loops.
- We design conditions where these emotions can arise.

## 2) Core Loop (Keep Simple Forever)

`Walk -> Reveal -> Feel -> Return`

Rules:
- This loop is the primary entry path.
- All other systems (quests, etudes, meridians) are advanced literacy layers.
- No layer may replace this loop as first contact.

## 3) Situated Creativity Law (Critical)

`Nothing created can exist without a place.`

Hard constraints:
- Any creation must be place-bound.
- No detached creation feed.
- No creation without embodied traversal.

This law protects ARCHÉ from drift into:
- social network
- content platform
- marketplace
- feed addiction system

## 4) Minimal Creative Primitives (Only)

Keep only these primitives in the engine:
- `inscription` (write)
- `link_places` (connect)
- `leave_question` (open meaning)
- `repeat_ritual` (transmit practice)
- `sequence_detection` (ghost path)

Anti-bloat rule:
- No editors, builders, or dashboards until behavior reveals necessity.

## 5) Constitutional Law: Meaning Requires Friction

`A trace is only valid if reality resisted its creation.`

At least one friction gate must be true:
- `Time friction`
  - Duration lived before validation.
- `Space friction`
  - Physical displacement required.
- `Existential friction`
  - The city may refuse.

Refusal protocol:
- Response remains opaque and non-explanatory.
- Canonical line: `The place remains closed.`
- No optimization signals.
- No progress hints.

Without this law, creation saturates into noise and memory collapses.
